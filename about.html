About

Access GCCCD coordinates accessibility work across the district.

It is a leadership and resource hub for employees, designed to support accessibility as an ongoing culture of shared responsibility.

Mission

Supporting accessible teaching, learning, technology, and communication across the Grossmont-Cuyamaca Community College District.

Brand Promise

Clear direction, trusted resources, timely updates, and practical tools that help employees understand their role, connect with the right people, and advance accessibility.

Everyone can lead.

Access GCCCD is built around the belief that accessibility leadership should be distributed rather than centralized.
