Roadmap

From discovery to sustained improvement.

The roadmap organizes accessibility work into phases that help the district move together.

1. Listen. Learn. Connect.

Discover current practices, identify pain points, map stakeholders, and find future champions.

2. Prioritize Together.

Use data to establish a hierarchy of needs and develop accountability mechanisms.

3. Build Capacity.

Publish templates, checklists, meeting structures, and resource pathways that help employees act.

4. Empower Action.

Support departments, offices, faculty, web editors, and staff as they update content and workflows.

5. Reflect & Evolve.

Review progress, revise priorities, celebrate champions, and continue improvement beyond April 2027.
